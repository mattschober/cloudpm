Problem: Logo not showing
Solution: 
  - Verify files are uploaded to the correct directory
  - Check filename spelling (case-sensitive on Linux servers)
  - Clear browser cache (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
  - Verify file permissions (should be 644)

Problem: Favicon not updating
Solution:
  - Clear browser cache completely
  - Try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+F5)
  - Wait 5-10 minutes for browser to update
  - Close and reopen browser

Problem: Logo too large or too small
Solution:
  - Resize your image file to recommended 200x200px
  - Or edit index.html and change "max-width: 200px" in .hero-logo
